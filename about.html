WAIT A MINUTE WHO ARE YOU?

My journey into the world of technology began with a spark ignited by Nier: Automata—a game that masterfully intertwines existential philosophy with immersive storytelling. This experience deepened my fascination with how narratives and interactivity can coexist, prompting me to pursue IT as a student. Beyond code, I channel creativity through strumming melodies on my guitar, exploring the rich tapestries of anime, and dissecting the nuances of literary classics. These passions collectively shape my perspective, blending analytical rigor with artistic sensibility.

As an aspiring software and web developer, I aim to craft solutions that transcend functionality. My goal is to build applications and digital experiences that serve as both practical tools and reflective mirrors—empowering users to navigate life's challenges while inviting introspection. Whether through sleek interfaces or dynamic systems, I strive to weave storytelling into technology, creating platforms that resonate on a human level and leave lasting impressions.
